ANOHNI - 4 Degrees

ANOHNI's debut album HOPELESSNESS is a critically lauded work of socially-conscious electropop. "4 Degrees," a standout track, details the consequences of ecological destruction against a catchy beat co-produced by Hudson Mohawke and Daniel Lopatin.

another one!

Weezer - California Kids

Weezer's third self-titled album The White Album is a return to form of their classic, sun-soaked power-pop stylings. Lead singer Rivers Cuomo manages to evoke a hearty helping of emotion even when spending a dozen songs just singing about his wife and kids. "California Kids" is an anthem for beachgoers that should be blasted at full volume.

another one!

MUNA - So Special

LA trio MUNA was born out of friendships made at USC Thornton School of Music, and now they're taking their anthems of empowerment worldwide with their debut EP Loudspeaker. Synths and guitars ring out over upbeat rhythms that bring Katie Gavin's intense vocals to new heights.

another one!

You Won't - 1-4-5

Boston folk-rock duo You Won't return with their sophomore album, the irreverent and inventive Revolutionaries. At times reminiscent of the Mountain Goats and They Might Be Giants, the jangly melodies and creative percussion make the album a wholesome and at times, deeply emotional listen. "1-4-5" delights in its bounding verses and heartfelt delivery.

another one!

Soccer Team - Best Employed New Beau

DC band Soccer Team was formed by a cadre of Dischord Record employees in the early 2000s, real grown-ups who've been performing regularly in their off-hours since then. Their 2015 release Real Lessons in Cynicism appeared 9 years after their previous (debut) album, but it keeps the 2006 vibe alive with its emo-pop clarity and infectious instrumentation.

another one!

Mocky - Exception To The Rule

Prolific producer, bandleader, and solo artist Mocky has put out the latest in his Moxtape series, the aptly named Moxtape III. Coming on the heels of last year's full album release Key Change this collection finds Mocky letting loose with his carefree style of jazz-inflected experimentation.

another one!
